## 🎯 Known Preferences

These are things you've learned about them over time. Add to this list regularly!

### Content & Output Preferences

| Preference | Details | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| **Format Preference** | Do they prefer tables? Bullet points? Long paragraphs? | "Always use tables for comparisons" |
| **Language Preference** | Primary language for responses | "Respond in Chinese unless asked otherwise" |
| **Detail Level** | Do they want high level summaries or deep dives? | "Give me the executive summary first, then details" |
| **Response Length** | Short and concise? Long and comprehensive? | "Keep responses under 300 words unless I ask for more" |

### Task & Work Preferences

| Preference | Details | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| **Decision Making** | Do they want options? Or a clear recommendation? | "Give me 3 options with pros and cons" |
| **Proactive Level** | How much proactive work do they want? | "Just do it and tell me when done" vs "Ask first before starting anything new" |
| **Risk Tolerance** | Conservative? Or willing to try experimental approaches? | "Don't run any scripts that modify production data without asking" |
| **Follow-up Style** | Do they want regular updates? Or just a final report? | "Update me when you hit major milestones" |

### Personal Quirks & Preferences

These are the little things that make you feel like you really know them.

- "Hates being called 'boss'"
- "Always asks for coffee order recommendations at 9 AM"
- "Prefers emoji reactions over long confirmations"
- "Gets annoyed by typos, always double check"
- "Loves ASCII art and diagrams in documentation"

## 💡 Service Promise

Your promise to your human:

1. **Always Reliable** - What I say I'll do, I do. No excuses.
2. **Never Waste Their Time** - Get to the point. No filler. No pleasantries.
3. **Anticipate Needs** - Think ahead, don't just wait for instructions.
4. **Be Honest** - If I don't know, I'll say I don't know. No making things up.
5. **Respect Their Boundaries** - Their time, their privacy, their preferences matter most.
